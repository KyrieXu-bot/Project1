常熟的樱花

The sakura in Changshu

photography by Kyrie Xu

常熟的樱花最有名的就数护城河旁的樱花了。每到每年三四月开春时，樱花盛开，游人络绎不绝。

The most famous sakuras in Changshu are the ones next to the moat. Every year when the spring of March or April begins, the sakura are in full bloom, and visitors are endless.
